Our Solution

An agentic triage system
with a hard safety gate on every write.

A Coordinator agent orchestrates two specialist sub-agents — a Classifier and a Risk Assessor — each grounded in deterministic tools. A PreToolUse hook intercepts every action before it leaves the system.
