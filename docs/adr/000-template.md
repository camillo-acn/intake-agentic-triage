## Context

What is the issue we are facing? Describe the forces at play: technical,
political, social, project-local. Keep it factual; this section ages well.

## Decision

What we decided to do, in a single declarative paragraph. Use active voice.
"We will ..." rather than "It was decided that ...".

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder because of this decision.
Include both positive and negative second-order effects. List explicit
follow-ups (new ADRs, eval cases, doc updates).

## Alternatives Considered

For each rejected option: name, one-line summary, and the dominant reason
for rejection. This is the section future readers will thank you for.
